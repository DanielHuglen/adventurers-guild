History

In the city of Neverwinter, four entrepreneurs sought to purchase a plot of land from the then-ruling queen of the Kingdom, Iselyn Neverember Colidar Drusar Sultanis De La Bouquè. They could but afford a humble abode in the least sought-after functioning district, but purchase it they did, flaws and all! Gathering up the rest of their funds, the adventurers-turned-capitalists bought the services of a handy dwarf named Glemrir Soots, whom together with their team renovated what was left of the house on the property.

After a brief séance with an evil wizard, the founding members found themselves saviours of the city, and were hailed as heroes of Neverwinter, complete with a statue in a square and everything! Using this newfound fame to market their new business venture, the group managed to snag several members right out of the gate. As soon as the new guildhouse was finished and opened, Eventyrerlauget was born!

From these humble and extremely relatable beginnings, Eventyrerlauget has grown to become a thriving guild of adventurers. Ever-growing, they seem to attract more and more adventurers into their fold. Most often by slaughtering their previous colleagues and offering a new steady income, but once in a while they actually save someone and convince them to join as well.

Today, Eventyrerlauget can boast a membership in the double digits, making them the largest adventuring guild in Neverwinter. Having run their competing guilds out of business, they now stand unchallenged as the premier choice for adventurers seeking work in the region. Some might say this monopoly is unhealthy for the local economy, but the founders of this glorious guild cannot hear those voices over all the gold they're making. Who knows what the future holds for these heroes? Probably more gold.
